FAQ

Common questions

When does Feast Planner launch?

We're targeting a beta launch in early 2026. Waitlist members get access first — and the first 100 get 3 months of Pro completely free.

Is there a free version?

Yes. Our Starter plan will always be free for up to 3 events per month. No credit card required to sign up.

How accurate is the AI scaling?

Very. Our scaling algorithm accounts for service style (buffet vs. plated), portion standards by food category, and waste factors. Most users find it more accurate than manual estimates.

Can I use my own recipes?

Absolutely. You can import recipes, build from scratch, or use our library. All scaling works the same regardless of the source.

Does it work for dietary restrictions?

Yes — gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, nut-free, and more. The AI flags allergens automatically and can suggest substitutions.

Can I share plans with my team?

Pro and Studio plans support team collaboration. Share event plans, assign prep tasks, and export documents for vendors and staff.
