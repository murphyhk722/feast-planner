Features

Everything you need to run the perfect event

One platform for menus, portions, costs, shopping, and staff — all powered by AI.

⚖️

AI Portion Scaling

Input your guest count and service style. Feast Planner instantly scales every ingredient in your menu with smart unit conversions.

Core AI
🧠

Smart Menu Builder

Describe your event and dietary needs — our AI suggests complete menus with balanced courses, allergies handled, and crowd favorites prioritized.

💰

Real-Time Cost Tracking

See your food cost per guest update live as you plan. Set a budget and Feast Planner flags when you're getting close.

🛒

Auto Shopping Lists

Exportable, aisle-organized shopping lists generated from your final menu. Share with your team or send to vendors in one click.

⏱

Prep Timeline Generator

Input your event start time and Feast Planner builds a backwards schedule — who does what, when, with zero conflicts.

📊

Event History & Insights

Track what worked across past events. Actual vs. estimated quantities, cost trends, and your most-loved dishes over time.
