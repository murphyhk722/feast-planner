Our Story

Built by someone who's been in the chaos

Feast Planner was born out of real frustration — too many spreadsheets, too many last-minute runs to Costco, and way too much time doing math that should just be done automatically.

The Founder

Hanna Murphy

Hi! I'm Hanna, a Mechanical Engineering student at Penn State with a passion for food, events, and technology. I grew up helping my family plan large holiday dinners and community fundraisers — and I watched the same problem happen every single time: someone would miscalculate the portions, we'd run out of food, or we'd end up with way too much of one thing.

When I started volunteering with a local catering company in college, I realized this wasn't just a family quirk — everyone struggles with event meal planning. Professional caterers were still using decade-old spreadsheets. Event planners were texting back and forth for hours trying to scale a recipe from 8 to 180. It was the kind of problem that practically begged for a better solution.

So I built one. Feast Planner is my attempt to give event planners and caterers the tool I always wished existed — something that handles the math, the logistics, and the chaos so you can focus on what actually matters: making great food and memorable events.
